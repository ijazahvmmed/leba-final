Laser Printer Film

A range of polyester based Laser Printable film for making positives majorly for exposing PS Plates, PCBs & Screen printing. Bundled with USPs of cost-effectiveness & simple-to-operate it is a value-for-money product replacing graphic arts films in select applications.

View Specifications

Key Features

  • • Superior top coat; for better toner adhesion & density
  • • Thermally stable base polyester; for better registration
  • • Universal compatibility; for ease & flexibility at workplace
  • • Daylight operations; for ease of handling
  • • Surface treatment; to prevent finger marks

Specifications

  • Base film: Polyester
  • Thickness: 100 microns
  • Coating: Single/Double side
  • Shelf life: One year from the date of packing when stored as recommended
  • Available sizes: 210 X 297(mm), 297 X 420(mm)
  • Recommended Storage: 20 - 25°C and 50% ± 5% (RH)
