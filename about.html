Industrial Excellence

At Leba Papers (Leba Trading Agencies), we are driven by a commitment to excellence in industrial speciality coatings and premium substrates. With over 39 years of experience, we have established ourselves as a leader in providing high-performance materials for luxury packaging, labeling, and graphic applications.

Our diverse portfolio includes specialized solutions for offset, flexo, and digital printing, catering to industries ranging from pharmaceuticals to high-end retail. We ensure that every sheet reflects our dedication to quality, innovation, and sustainability.

With a robust infrastructure spanning 100,000 sq. ft. in Kerala, we maintain rigorous inventory controls and high-speed processing capabilities to serve our clients efficiently. Our strategic presence in Ernakulam and Thrissur allows us to deliver custom solutions that meet the evolving needs of the market.

We combine industrial reliability with aesthetic excellence, ensuring our partners have access to the finest materials for their creative and functional requirements.
