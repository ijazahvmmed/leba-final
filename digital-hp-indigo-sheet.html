Digital Print Media / Press Media

HP Indigo (Sheet Fed)

A range of single or both side pre-primed polyester films ideal for HP Indigo digital presses. Suitable for use in photo books,wedding albums & commercial applications like Certificates, Visiting Cards, Menu Cards and Brochures etc.

View Specifications

Key Features

  • • Patented coating for superior ink adhesion
  • • Excellent water resistance and weatherability
  • • Excellent shade consistency
  • • High opacity (>97%) for white opaque films for both side printing
  • • Excellent gloss and print sharpness
  • • Good anti-static properties for trouble-free printing
  • • Dimensionally stable film suitable for thermal lamination

Specifications

  • Base film: Polyester
  • Surface finish: Matte/Glossy/Satin
  • Shelf life: One years from the date of packing, when stored as recommended
  • Available sizes: 330 X 482 (mm), 320 X 470 (mm) , 530 X 750 (mm)
  • Recommended storage: 20 - 25°C and 50% ± 5% (RH)
